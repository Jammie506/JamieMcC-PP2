Taking the Exam

Time

You will have 20 minutes to complete this exam. The page will automatically time you so that you can see how much time you have remaining.

Questions

There will be 10 questions on the exam, each of them relating to something that we have covered in class. Be sure to read the questions carefully.
Each question is multiple choice, so be sure to choose the correct answer each time.

Starting the Exam

To start the exam all you need to do is click over to the "Exam" and hit "Start Exam", this will start your timer.
Don't rush, take your time and go over everything.
